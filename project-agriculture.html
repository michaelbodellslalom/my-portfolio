Project Overview

Led an end-to-end UX engagement for a financial hub experience serving growers and crop sales teams. The work moved from field research through tested design concepts and into an execution-ready implementation plan.

End-to-End UX Process

The project followed a full UX lifecycle: plan research, conduct on-site interviews, synthesize findings, define recommendations, design solutions, validate through usability testing, and create an implementation plan for rollout.

Interview Planning and Field Research

Created structured interview guides tailored to each audience, then traveled to multiple locations throughout the country to meet participants on their farms and in their field sales offices.

  • Developed interview guides for farmers, crop sales representatives, and client teams
  • Conducted on-site interviews and contextual inquiry to observe real customer experiences
  • Captured pain points across enrollment, program tracking, and decision-making tasks
  • Documented language, mental models, and constraints specific to each customer group

Research Synthesis

Converted qualitative field data into clear themes and decision-ready insights.

  • Clustered findings into behavioral themes, friction points, and unmet needs
  • Mapped current-state journeys and handoffs across stakeholder roles
  • Identified high-impact opportunities to reduce complexity and improve clarity

Journey Map

We created a visual model of how customers move through the experience over time. We captured each stage of the process, customer goals, actions, emotions, and pain points so teams could see where friction happens and where improvements would have the greatest impact.

Design and Prototyping

Translated research and interview learnings into a set of customer-centered designs showing future-state experience flows.

  • Created workflow concepts for enrollment, account management, and insights dashboards
  • Built prototype flows to communicate interaction patterns and information hierarchy
  • Aligned design decisions to the needs of farmers and sales teams

Usability Testing

Tested proposed designs with stakeholders to gather feedback and improve clarity before implementation.

  • Facilitated usability sessions with representative customers and stakeholder groups
  • Measured comprehension, task completion, and navigation confidence
  • Iterated designs based on feedback to reduce friction and improve decision support

Implementation Plan

Created a practical implementation plan to guide rollout of the new designs across teams.

  • Defined phased delivery milestones and dependencies
  • Outlined ownership and handoff points across product, design, and development
  • Provided guidance for adoption and measurement after release

Deliverables

  • Interview guides and field research artifacts
  • Research synthesis and opportunity framework
  • Prioritized UX recommendations
  • Future-state designs and prototype flows
  • Usability testing insights and design iterations
  • Implementation roadmap and execution guidance

Impact

This end-to-end UX process aligned stakeholders around validated customer needs, improved confidence in the proposed experience direction, and delivered a clear path to implement new designs that improve the grower's brand trust, increasing annual spend and retention while also improving the crop sales team's ability to convert.
