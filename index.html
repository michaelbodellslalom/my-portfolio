About Me

I'm a UX Designer specializing in design, research, and strategy, with a focus on visual UI design for apps, websites, and tools. I'm deeply curious about human behavior, and I'm passionate about crafting end-to-end user experiences—from discovery through to living products and solutions. I thrive on translating complex challenges into intuitive, user-centered designs that drive real business impact.

My experience spans consulting, experience design, and account management across diverse industries including e-commerce, telecommunications, finance, tech, public sector, commercial real estate, aerospace, and agriculture.
